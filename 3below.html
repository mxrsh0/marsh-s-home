About the Series

3Below follows royal siblings Aja and Krel Tarron as they escape their war-torn home planet of Akiridion-5 and seek refuge on Earth. While hiding in Arcadia Oaks, they forge unlikely friendships, learn what it means to be human, and prepare for the battle to reclaim their kingdom.
