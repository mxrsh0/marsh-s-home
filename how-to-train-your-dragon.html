Synopsis

Long ago on the island of Berk, Vikings and dragons have been enemies for generations. Hiccup Horrendous Haddock III, the son of Chief Stoick the Vast, struggles to fit into Viking life. Everything changes when he encounters an injured Night Fury dragon and forms an unlikely friendship with Toothless. Together they challenge centuries of tradition, discover the true nature of dragons, and prove that courage is found not in fighting, but in understanding.
