About this Collection

Welcome to the Arcadia Archives. This collection brings together every chapter of the Tales of Arcadia universe, including Trollhunters, 3Below, Wizards and the concluding feature film, Rise of the Titans. Each story has been organised into one complete archive so you can easily explore the world of Arcadia Oaks from beginning to end.

Viewing Order

Although each series introduces new heroes and perspectives, every chapter tells one connected story. For the best experience, this archive follows the recommended chronological order, allowing you to watch the saga exactly as it unfolds before reaching its epic conclusion.
