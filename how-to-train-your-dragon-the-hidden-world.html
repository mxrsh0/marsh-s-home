Synopsis

As dragons around the world are hunted by ruthless dragon trappers, Hiccup and Toothless discover the existence of the legendary Hidden World, a secret sanctuary where dragons can live in peace. When Toothless encounters a rare Light Fury, their bond is tested as both dragon and rider are forced to make life-changing decisions about the future of Berk. The final chapter of the trilogy explores friendship, leadership, and learning when it is time to let go.
