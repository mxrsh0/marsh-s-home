About the Film

Rise of the Titans serves as the epic conclusion to the Tales of Arcadia trilogy, bringing together the heroes of Trollhunters, 3Below and Wizards for one final battle against the Arcane Order. As ancient Titans awaken across the world, Jim Lake Jr. and his allies must unite to protect both humanity and the magical realm in DreamWorks' climactic finale.
