Synopsis

Gobber gathers Hiccup and the Dragon Riders around a roaring fire to share the unbelievable story of his obsession with tracking down the legendary Boneknapper Dragon. As the tale unfolds, the line between fact and fiction becomes increasingly difficult to tell, resulting in one of the franchise's most entertaining and humorous adventures.
