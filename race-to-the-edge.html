About the Series

Race to the Edge follows Hiccup, Toothless and the Dragon Riders as they journey far beyond Berk to explore the Dragon Eye, uncover ancient dragon secrets and confront powerful enemies including Viggo Grimborn, Krogan and the Dragon Hunters. Set before the events of How to Train Your Dragon 2, the series expands the Dragon world like never before.
